Rutgers Persian Club

A community for Persian culture, student connection, campus events, and celebrating heritage at Rutgers. Meet new people, enjoy food and music, and help build a strong cultural presence on campus.

Welcome to the Club

The Rutgers Persian Club brings students together through cultural events, social gatherings, educational discussions, and campus involvement. Whether you are Persian or just interested in the culture, this club is a place to connect, learn, and be part of something meaningful.

Student Community • Culture • Events • Rutgers

What We Do

We host meetups, cultural celebrations, discussion nights, and social events that help students build friendships and stay connected to Persian identity and traditions.

Club Highlights

  • Persian New Year celebrations
  • Community dinners and tea nights
  • Music, poetry, and cultural showcases
  • Networking with students across campus

Leadership

Guided by dedicated student leadership, the club focuses on building a welcoming space for culture, community, and school spirit.
